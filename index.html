Publications

    Bandwidth based Heavily Loaded Lightpath Protection for IP/MPLS over Optical Networks

    This paper addresses the optimization of survivability in IP/MPLS-over-Optical WDM networks, proposing a bandwidth-based approach to heavy lightpath protection. Simulation experiments demonstrate significant reduction in bandwidth wastage with manageable levels of blocking, making the proposed scheme highly effective for network providers.

    Authors: K. Ratnam, K.M.A.S. Kulathunga, L.T.R.J. Prabodhana, P.M.M.N. Pathiraja, Y.M.T.L. Yapa
